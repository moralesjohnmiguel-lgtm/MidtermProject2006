About Me

Hi, I'm John Miguel Barrientos Morales. I'm currently a 2nd Year BSIT student at University of the East - Manila. I was born in Calamba, Laguna, and raised in Pangil, Laguna. I have two siblings, and I am the youngest of the three of us. My mother is a stay-at-home mom who manages our apartment, while my father is an OFW in Abu Dhabi and is still currently working there.

I have two siblings: my older sister, who is the first child; my older brother, who is the second child; and lastly, me, the youngest of the three. I graduated from Pangil Elementary School and Siniloan Integrated National High School before continuing my college years at the University of the East.
